About Us

Our Backstory

The women of Sirius Psychics and Clairvoyants, a tight-knit group of spiritual guides and mystics, first met 37 years ago at a concert in Chicago, an event that would unknowingly become a pivotal moment in their lives. The year was 1987, and the city was alive with energy, bustling with music, art, and the growing interest in the metaphysical world. Each woman came from a different background but shared a deep connection to the mystical arts, unknowingly drawn to the same venue that night for reasons beyond music.

As the concert buzzed with the energy of the crowd, a seemingly coincidental moment brought them together. Whether it was a chance meeting in the lobby, a shared interest in the artist performing, or a mystical alignment of the stars, the bond between them formed instantly. It wasn’t just a love for music or a shared experience, but a recognition of something much deeper—an acknowledgment of their shared gifts and purpose.

In the months following their serendipitous meeting, the women began to explore their unique spiritual abilities together. Some were tarot readers, others practiced mediumship, crystal healing, or astrology. Each brought their own wisdom, but together, they formed a powerful collective, channeling energies and insights that seemed to grow stronger with their unity. The bond they shared became the foundation of Sirius Psychics and Clairvoyants, a group dedicated to helping others tap into their intuition and spiritual potential.

For nearly four decades, the women of Sirius have guided countless people on their spiritual journeys, offering psychic readings, healing sessions, and deep insights into the mysteries of life. They became known not only for their accuracy and compassion but also for the unshakable sisterhood that bound them together, rooted in that fateful concert in Chicago where the universe brought them together.

Over the years, their paths intertwined with major spiritual movements, and their work evolved with the times, but the connection between them remained unbroken. Today, their legacy continues, as they pass down their wisdom to a new generation of mystics and psychics, ensuring that the magic that brought them together 37 years ago continues to flourish.
